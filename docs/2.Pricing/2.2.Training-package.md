# **Training package plans**

---

The training package provides support for bioinformatics skills development, capacity building initiatives, workshops and tutorials.

## **Starter pack**
A dedicated training account including:

+ Minimum of 10 participants (access to 4vCPUs each)
+ 500GB Team Shared Storage
+ 1TB S3 Storage
+ 1 week early access for setup

| Resource | Price until 31st March 2026 | Price from 1st April 2026 |
|---| ----------- | ---- |
| Starter pack | starts at **£30 per participant per week** excl. VAT | starts at **£35 per participant per week** excl. VAT |

A Starter pack can be expanded to fit exactly what resources you require for your training.
